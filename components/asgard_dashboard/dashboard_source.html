Universal Building Physics
Avg Room Temp?°CAverage room temperature yesterday. Together with outside temp, determines heat loss coefficient.
Delta Room Temp?°C (daily swing)Difference between max and min room temp yesterday. Small delta = high thermal mass (house holds heat well).
HL × TM Product?hours (Tau)Thermal time constant of your house. Higher = house holds heat longer. Learned automatically. Typical: 50–300h.
Passive Solar Factor?kWh per W/m² irradianceHeat entering through windows from solar irradiance. Independent of PV panels. Typical: 0.010–0.030. Learned automatically.
Battery SoC?kWhCurrent battery state of charge. Solver assigns discharge to most expensive HP hours. Set 0 if no battery.
Battery Max Discharge?kWMaximum discharge rate of your battery. Limits how much power solver can assign per hour.
